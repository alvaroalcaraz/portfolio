10 years after I was already being living not only in Barcelona but London, United Kindom, Strasbourg, France and Charleroi, Belgium and I decided to study UX/UI Design, I already had a lot of experience in Tourism and Customer service and that help me empathize more with the users and make make work harder to make their lives easier.
